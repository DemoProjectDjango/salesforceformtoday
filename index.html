Why Google APIs?

We use Google APIs strictly to facilitate the connection between your Forms and Salesforce.

  • Authenticate via OAuth 2.0
  • List Forms owned by you
  • Read form structure & questions
  • Retrieve responses securely

OAuth Scopes Justification

Requested scopes are minimal and strictly necessary:

  • Google Forms: Read access to fetch questions and answers.
  • Google Drive: Limited access strictly to locate your Forms files.
Scopes are used only after explicit consent during active sync.

No Sensitive or Restricted Usage

  • Does NOT read Gmail
  • Does NOT send emails
  • Does NOT show ads
  • Does NOT track users
  • Does NOT sell or share data

User Control

User-Initiated Action Only

All API calls are triggered by manual user interactions inside Salesforce. There are no background or silent data pulls.

Target Users: Salesforce Administrators, Business Users, and Organizations.
